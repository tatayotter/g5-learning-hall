Installing the APK

  1. Tap Download LearningHall.apk above and let it finish downloading.
  2. Open the downloaded file from your notifications or Downloads folder.
  3. If Android warns about "unknown sources," tap Settings in that prompt and allow installs from your browser/file app — this is normal for apps outside the Play Store.
  4. Tap Install, then open Learning Hall from your app drawer.

New builds are published automatically — reinstalling later gets you the latest version.
